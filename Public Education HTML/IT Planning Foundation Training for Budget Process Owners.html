IT Planning Foundation Series (updated to v1.56)

  • Introduction - 12 min
    • Recognize the user roles in IT Planning Foundation
    • Follow the workflow used for planning in IT Planning Foundation
    • Navigate the IT Planning Foundation interface
    • Analyze information displayed in the Summary View

  • Budget Planning - 8 min
    • Set or review targets
    • Analyze a budget
    • Enter budget data
    • Collaborate on a budget
    • Review and approve or return a budget
    • Submit a budget

  • Essentials for BPOs and Group CCOs - 8 min
    • Create a budget or forecast
    • Set and publish targets
    • Adjust baselines
    • Open a plan
    • Check status and approve or return plans
    • Finalize a plan / Reopen a Plan
    • Import Actuals
    • Multi-Year Planning

  • Forecasting and Spend Management - 5 min
    • Analyze a forecast
    • Compare actuals to a budget
    • Enter forecast data
    • Review and approve or return forecasts
    • Submit a forecast

  • Labor Resource Planning - 7 min
    • Turn on labor planning
    • Load labor roles and rates
    • Set labor allocation rules
    • Set labor targets
    • Enter headcount
    • Review auto-filled GL accounts

  • Entering Plan Data - 18 min
    • Show and hide columns
    • Freeze columns
    • Group columns
    • Add new line items
    • Export and Import plans
    • Compare plans or versions of plans
    • Review and adjust a Labor plan

  • Contract Planning - 6 min
    • Turn on contract planning
    • Define contract types to align with the GL account codes
    • Enter contract details
    • View line items generated in the OpEx budget related to the entered contracts
    • View the composition and comparison of contracts data

  • Asset Planning - 7 min
    • Turn on asset planning
    • Setup asset classes that specify the depreciation and accounting treatment for different types of assets
    • Enter existing and planned asset details
    • View line items generated in the OpEx and CapEx budgets related to capitalized assets
    • View the composition and comparison of assets data
